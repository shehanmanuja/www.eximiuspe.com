Energy Storage Systems

Our liquid-cooled LFP battery energy storage systems (BESS) are engineered for peak shaving, overnight cycle loads, and backup security during grid instability events.

  • Liquid-cooled LFP cell chemistry — safest for tropical climates
  • Peak shaving to reduce maximum demand charges

  • Seamless grid backup with <20ms switchover
