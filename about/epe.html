Engineering Heritage

Powering the Nation Sustainably

**Eximius Power & Energy (Private) Limited (EPE)** was established in 2023 as a specialized subsidiary of Eximius (Private) Limited, which has been operating as a prominent electrical engineering and installation firm since 2014.

Leveraging our parent company's extensive heritage in electrical grid construction and switchboard manufacturing, EPE was created to deliver turnkey solar PV installations, micro-grids, and advanced energy storage systems (BESS).

We operate as an Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) contractor, managing complex utility connections and structural engineering to ensure long-term returns on green energy assets.

Corporate Mandates

OUR VISION

To transition Sri Lanka’s energy grid to a resilient, zero-carbon network powered by intelligent solar resources and energy storage networks.

OUR MISSION

To deliver premium renewable energy assets to industrial, C&I, and residential markets using high-tech engineering designs and strict component quality.

COMPLIANCE CREDENTIALS

EPE is a registered solar PV service provider with the **Sri Lanka Sustainable Energy Authority (SLSEA)** under registration coordinate **SLSEA/PV/2023/108**.
