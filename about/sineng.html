Global Partner

Sineng Electric Co., Ltd.

**Sineng Electric Co., Ltd.** is a global leading supplier of solar inverters and energy storage solutions (BESS). Established in 2012 and publicly listed on the Shenzhen Stock Exchange (SZSE: 300827), Sineng has built a reputation for high-power electronics engineering.

Ranked consistently in the **Top 10 Global Inverter Manufacturers** by Wood Mackenzie, Sineng provides central, string, and hybrid inverters, along with battery storage cabinets.

Through our partnership in Sri Lanka, EPE supplies Sineng's utility-scale inverters (up to 350kW) and energy storage containers, backed by official warranties.
