Thermal Optimization

Why Liquid Cooling Matters?

Standard battery backup structures suffer thermal stress during charging cycles in Sri Lankan high-temperature conditions. Liquid cooling circulates coolant loops directly through cell plates, reducing cell temperature variation to less than 2°C.

  • ✔ Extends total battery lifespan by 20% compared to air-cooled systems.
  • ✔ Prevents localized hot-spots, eliminating fire/thermal runway risks.
  • ✔ Reduces internal auxiliary power consumption by up to 30%.
Peak Shaving Savings: Commercial entities in Sri Lanka can run on battery power during Peak Hours (6:30 PM - 10:30 PM) to avoid high peak electricity tariffs, realizing immediate operational savings.
