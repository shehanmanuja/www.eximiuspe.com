EPC Framework

Our 5-Step Turnkey Installation Process

Eximius Power & Energy (EPE) executes utility-grade Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) protocols. We handle the entire engineering pipeline, ensuring that industrial, commercial, and residential clients transition smoothly to solar power.

Each installation is engineered to meet strict CEB/LECO electrical protection parameters, securing approval for Net-Metering, Net-Accounting, or Net-Plus schemes.

Calculate System Sizing
01

Feasibility Audit & 3D Shading Modeling

We conduct site surveys and use 3D modeling tools to simulate year-round shading profiles from adjacent trees or buildings, ensuring panels are placed at optimal angles.

02

System Electrical Engineering

Our electrical engineers design the wiring architecture, select cable sizes, and match inverter MPPT inputs to panel strings, incorporating robust surge and ground protection.

03

Tier 1 Quality Procurement

We secure high-performance Tier-1 Bloomberg rated bifacial PV panels, high-efficiency Sineng smart inverters, and hot-dip galvanized mounting structures that resist coastal corrosion.

04

Precision On-Site Commissioning

Certified technicians assemble structural frames, install panels, run DC/AC cabling, configure isolation systems, and perform insulation resistance testing.

05

Grid Synchronization & Live Activation

We coordinate with utility inspectors (CEB/LECO) to configure net-meters, execute final testing, sync the Sineng inverters to grid frequency, and activate your cloud-monitoring dashboard.
