Yield Optimization

Securing Energy Yields for Decades

A solar PV system is a long-term investment designed to run for 25+ years. Dust accumulation, shading, panel degradation, or connection issues can reduce energy production. EPE’s specialized Operations & Maintenance (O&M) division provides active monitoring and onsite servicing.

Through our real-time cloud diagnostic networks, our control room detects inverter errors and system drops, sending field teams to inspect issues before they impact electricity savings.

Preventive Maintenance Checklist

  • [✓]
    Pressure Panel Cleaning: Removing dust, soot, and bird droppings using pure chemical-free water to prevent cell damage.
  • [✓]
    Thermal Imaging Scans: Using specialized infrared cameras to identify hot-spots and micro-cracks in solar cells.
  • [✓]
    Electrical Grid Audits: Checking grounding systems, measuring cable insulation, and testing terminal torque parameters.
  • [✓]
    Inverter Firmware Updates: Running regular updates on Sineng smart inverters to optimize grid compatibility.
